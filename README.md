# 🌍 Overview

AeroGuard AI is an **AI-powered Predictive Maintenance Intelligence Platform** that predicts the **Remaining Useful Life (RUL)** of aircraft engines using machine learning.

Instead of waiting for machinery to fail or performing maintenance at fixed schedules, AeroGuard AI continuously analyzes engine sensor data and forecasts equipment degradation, enabling proactive maintenance decisions.

The platform combines:

- Machine Learning
- Explainable AI (SHAP)
- Fleet Health Monitoring
- Intelligent Maintenance Recommendations
- Business Impact Analysis
- Interactive Dashboard
- AI Maintenance Copilot

to help maintenance engineers reduce downtime, improve reliability, and optimize maintenance costs.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Unexpected machinery failures are among the biggest causes of financial losses in industries such as aerospace, manufacturing, and heavy engineering.

Traditional maintenance strategies suffer from major limitations.

## Reactive Maintenance

Maintenance is performed only after equipment fails.

### Problems

- Unexpected downtime
- High repair costs
- Production losses
- Reduced operational efficiency
- Increased safety risks

---

## Preventive Maintenance

Maintenance is performed at fixed intervals regardless of the machine's actual health.

### Problems

- Unnecessary servicing
- Resource wastage
- Higher maintenance cost
- Reduced equipment availability

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

AeroGuard AI introduces an AI-driven predictive maintenance framework that forecasts engine failures before they occur.

The system processes real-world aircraft sensor telemetry and predicts the Remaining Useful Life (RUL) of engines.

Based on the predicted health condition, AeroGuard AI automatically generates:

- Remaining Useful Life (RUL)
- Health Score
- Failure Risk Level
- Maintenance Priority
- Recommended Maintenance Actions
- Explainable AI Insights
- Business Impact Analysis

This allows organizations to transition from reactive maintenance to intelligent predictive maintenance.

# 📚 Dataset

## NASA CMAPSS Turbofan Engine Dataset

AeroGuard AI uses the **NASA Commercial Modular Aero-Propulsion System Simulation (CMAPSS)** dataset.

The dataset contains simulated aircraft engine degradation data collected over multiple operational cycles.

### Dataset Includes

- Engine ID
- Operational Cycle
- Operational Settings
- 21 Sensor Measurements
- Remaining Useful Life (RUL)

The CMAPSS dataset is one of the most widely used benchmark datasets for Predictive Maintenance and Remaining Useful Life prediction.

# 🧠 Explainable AI (XAI)

One of the biggest challenges in Machine Learning is understanding **why** a model made a particular prediction.

AeroGuard AI addresses this challenge by integrating **SHAP (SHapley Additive Explanations)**.

Instead of acting like a black box, the system provides transparent explanations for every prediction.

---

## Why Explainability Matters

In safety-critical industries such as aerospace, engineers cannot rely solely on prediction values.

They need to understand:

- Why the engine is predicted to fail
- Which sensors contributed most
- Which components require attention
- How confident the prediction is

SHAP provides these insights, enabling engineers to make informed maintenance decisions.

---

## SHAP Features

- Local Feature Importance
- Global Feature Importance
- Waterfall Plot
- Feature Contribution Analysis
- Positive & Negative Sensor Impact

- Robust against noisy sensor data

- Widely adopted in predictive maintenance research
